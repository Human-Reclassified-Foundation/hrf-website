Our Mission

The Human Reclassified Foundation is a nonprofit dedicated to educating and empowering people to navigate the ethical, social, and personal impacts of artificial intelligence. We use immersive media, cinematic storytelling, and community-focused workshops to make complex tech understandable—and help ensure it works for humanity, not against it.

Through our content and outreach, we champion transparency, inclusion, fairness, and ethical development in AI systems that affect everything from justice and privacy to daily decision-making.

Upcoming Program: AI Scam Prevention for Seniors

We're developing an empowering workshop series to help seniors learn how to spot scams using AI tools. This free public program will combine hands-on learning with easy-to-use AI apps that detect phishing, fraud, and suspicious activity. No tech background required—just bring your curiosity!
